Villa Okinrose

Villa Okinrose in Unawatuna features a garden and a terrace. The property is located 6.4 km from Japanese Peace Pagoda and 3.1 km from Yatagala Temple. Dalawella Beach is a 9-minute walk from the resort and Mihiripenna Beach is half a kilometer away. 

At the resort, every room is equipped with a desk. The rooms come with a private bathroom and free toiletries, and certain rooms at Villa Okinrose have a balcony. The rooms at the accommodation come with a seating area. 

Around the clock assistance is available at the reception. 

Unawatuna Beach is 2.7 km from Villa Okinrose. 

This property is also rated for the best value in Unawatuna! Guests are getting more for their money when compared to other properties in this city.
